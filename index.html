About Us

Crazy Babies is a play-to-earn gaming metaverse, with a private collection of 7.777 Crazy Babies NFT. Crazy babies are the playable characters of the game, and each one is a combination of over 200 hand-drawn traits and algorithmically generated attributes, with a few Crazy Baby collectibles only occurring once. The Crazy babies are an ERC-721 digital collectible.

Owning a Crazy Babies NFT comes with a membership to a private group, exclusively created for Crazy Babies collectors. There are also multiple benefits and exclusive utilities that ONLY members get to enjoy.
